dark academia is an aesthetic that revolves around classic literature, the pursuit of self-discovery, and a general passion for knowledge and learning. dark academia's best-known visual variations stem primarily from european cultures.



gen z has reclaimed dark academia and has built a growing community on social media, alongside other budding aesthetics like cottagecore, grandmacore, goblincore and light academia.
